Our Story

Founded in 2003 by John Ragucci, Ragucci Construction began as a small family business with a big vision: to bring resort-quality pools to New Hampshire homes. With 20+ years of experience, we’ve grown into a trusted name in pool construction, known for our attention to detail and commitment to excellence. Our mission is simple—turn your backyard into a space where memories are made.
